The Student Investment Fund
at the California Institute of Technology

Who we are

The Student Investment Fund (SIF) is an organization at the California Institute of Technology responsible for manging and investing about $700,000 of Caltech's endowment. We welcome all students, faculty, and staff who have an interest in investing and financial markets, regardless of knowledge or experience. There is no financial obligation whatsoever on the part of club members.

What we do

SIF holds weekly meetings to discuss market activity, portfolio performance, and investment strategies. Our meetings are open to anyone who wishes to familiarize themselves with investing, join the club, or simply hear our take on the past week's major news items. Our meetings are held at 6:30 p.m. on Wednesdays in the first floor Conference Room in Schlinger Laboratory (Room 101). Note: Meetings are currently held biweekly due to decreased availability over summer.

How to join

If you would like to subscribe to our email list, please click here for instructions. Upon subscribing, you will receive e-mails pertaining to general SIF activites including trade confirmations, meeting time or date changes, and urgent trade motions. If you would also like to subscribe to our more general discussion listserv, please click here. You may remove yourself from either or both lists at any time.
